A · Okabe–Ito

Color-blind safe standard. 8 hues, all distinguishable in deuteranopia/protanopia/tritanopia. Most-cited palette in Nature/Science figures.

B · Tableau 10 (Muted)

Dense, quiet, no neon. Works on white and dark backgrounds. Good for 4+ series without visual fatigue. IEEE Transactions–compatible.

C · Paired L/R

Semantic: Left limb = cool blue, Right limb = warm red. Desired = solid, Actual = dashed. Print & greyscale safe because hue carries side, dash carries signal type.

D · Viridis-accent

Brand orange stays only for Target / Actual. Everything else uses perceptually-uniform Viridis steps. Great when you have a gradient-by-trial scenario.
