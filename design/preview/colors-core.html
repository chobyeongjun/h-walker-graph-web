Four colors. Each one has exactly one job. Navy carries depth, orange carries action, mint carries life, purple-navy carries panels. Nothing else enters the palette — restraint is the brand.
Foundation Navy 950 The app canvas. Always present, never competes — orange and mint pop because this is deep.

Layering rule: #0B0E2E navy is the app canvas — nothing else can claim it. #171B5E purple-navy sits on top at varying alpha (12/28/55%) to build four depth tiers. #F09708 orange owns everything a user can act on — CTAs, logo, focus rings, eyebrow labels. #00FFB2 mint is the single "this is alive" color — LIVE dots, streaming responses, data-peak markers. Never mix orange and mint on the same element; they're deliberately opposite on the color wheel so they always stay distinct.
